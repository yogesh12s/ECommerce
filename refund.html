Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: 29 November 2025

1. Eligibility for Returns

We accept returns within 7–14 days of delivery depending on your region. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and include all accessories and tags.

2. Non-Returnable Items

Certain products cannot be returned for hygiene or safety reasons, including:

  • Beauty accessories
  • Personal hygiene items
  • Custom-made or personalized items

3. Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will notify you. Approved refunds will be issued within 7–14 business days to your original payment method.

4. Late or Missing Refunds

If your refund is delayed, first check your bank or payment provider. If you still have not received your refund, contact us.

5. Exchanges

Exchanges are only accepted for defective or damaged items. Contact us for assistance before sending an item back.

6. Shipping Costs

Return shipping fees are the responsibility of the customer unless the item arrived damaged or incorrect.

7. Contact Us

For return or refund requests, email: support@yourstore.example
